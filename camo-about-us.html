    About Us


    Built on Faith - Powered by Family - Proven in Work

    At Camo Electric, we treat every home and business like our own. Rooted in family values and faith, we bring honesty, safety, and quality workmanship to every project. From Pawnee to the Tulsa metro and rural towns across Oklahoma, and with 30+ years of combined experience, and 10+ years in business, our mission is simple: provide dependable service, peace of mind, and lasting trust for the families and communities we serve.



    Miguel Gonzalez

    Miguel is the founder and driving force behind Camo Electric. With over a decade of dedication to building the company and a lifetime of experience in the electrical field, his passion for the trade started as a young boy and grew into a career rooted in hard work, integrity, and service.

    As both a skilled electrician and a pillar in the community, Miguel leads by example, bringing determination, faith, and family values into every aspect of the business. Under his leadership, our licensed technicians arrive on time, in uniform, and fully prepared to handle both scheduled and emergency service. From troubleshooting to preventive maintenance, Miguel’s standard of excellence ensures Camo Electric continues to provide reliable support that keeps homes and businesses across Oklahoma safe and operational.

    Amy Gonzalez

    Amy is the heart behind the scenes at Camo Electric. While Miguel leads in the field, Amy manages the administrative side of the business, ensuring everything runs smoothly and efficiently. From handling day-to-day operations and organizing paperwork to assisting Miguel with projects and providing constant support, she plays a vital role in keeping the company strong.

    Her commitment, organization, and dedication reflect the same faith and family values that define Camo Electric. Amy’s behind-the-scenes efforts allow the team to focus on delivering safe, reliable service to homes and businesses across Oklahoma, while her support for Miguel helps drive the company forward with integrity and excellence.

    Phillip Brown

    Phillip is Camo Electric’s #1 journeyman and Miguel’s go-to man for the Tulsa metro. With years of hands-on experience and a deep knowledge of the trade, he brings skill, reliability, and leadership to every project he touches. Whether it’s overseeing complex jobs, mentoring newer technicians, or making sure customers receive top-notch service, Phillip is trusted to get the job done right.

    His work ethic and commitment to excellence reflect the same values that Camo Electric was built on faith, family, and integrity. As the company’s lead presence in the Tulsa metro, Phillip plays a vital role in delivering the quality and dependability that Camo Electric is known for across Oklahoma.
